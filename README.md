# CoopQueue

CoopQueue is an English editorial website for players trying to choose the next co-op game after breakout hits such as R.E.P.O., Lethal Company, Content Warning, and PEAK.

It is designed for players who already know the kind of group energy they want, but still need help deciding which game to buy or boot next.

## What the site covers

- recommendation lists for different moods, budgets, and group sizes
- "games like" pages for readers starting from a favorite game
- comparison features that explain tradeoffs between similar next picks
- plain-English explainers for mechanics, labels, and emerging co-op terms

## Main sections

- `Best`: broader shortlist pages for chaotic co-op, cheap co-op horror, proximity chat horror, and Discord-night picks
- `Games Like`: recommendation routes built around a known anchor game, especially R.E.P.O. and Lethal Company
- `Features`: tighter editorial pages focused on alternatives, fit, and game-to-game differences
- `Explained`: short guides that unpack why certain co-op experiences feel funny, tense, messy, or replayable

## Editorial focus

- next-game decisions after breakout co-op hits
- physics-driven horror and visible teamwork mistakes
- voice chat tension, proximity systems, and social panic
- practical recommendation writing instead of generic game coverage

## Reader promise

The site helps readers move from vague intent like "something like R.E.P.O." or "what should we play after Lethal Company?" to a clear next pick with enough context to decide quickly.
